Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2024

1. Cancellation by Rider

Riders can cancel a ride request for free within 2 minutes of the driver accepting the trip. If cancelled after 2 minutes, a nominal cancellation fee may apply.

2. Cancellation by Driver

If a driver cancels after reaching the pickup location, the rider will not be charged.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are processed for technical errors, double charges, or if a service was not provided despite payment. All refund requests must be raised within 24 hours of the trip.

4. Process

Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days via Razorpay.

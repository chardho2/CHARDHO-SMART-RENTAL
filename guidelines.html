Dos & Don'ts

Essential guidelines for a smooth Chardho experience.

The Dos

Arrive 10m Early

Punctuality is the hallmark of luxury service. Always be at the pickup point before the scheduled time.

Maintain Cleanliness

Ensure the vehicle is vacuumed and smelling fresh before every trip assignment.

Dress Professionally

Wear the Chardho representative attire to maintain a premium brand image.

Check Vehicle Health

Perform a quick inspection of tires, fuel, and fluid levels before going online.

The Don'ts

Internal Communication

Never share personal contact details with customers. All communication must happen through the app.

Speeding & Recklessness

Strictly follow speed limits. Safety is non-negotiable in our ecosystem.

Smoking/Eating

Do not smoke or eat inside the car. Maintain a clinical environment for the guests.

Unauthorized Passengers

Do not carry any unauthorized passengers during an active trip assignment.

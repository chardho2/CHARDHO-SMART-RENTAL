Customer Do’s & Don’ts

Do’s

  • Book your vehicle well in advance to ensure availability.
  • Inspect the car for any existing damage before driving.
  • Return the vehicle with a full tank of fuel as per the rental agreement.
  • Report any issues or accidents to our support team immediately.
  • Respect traffic laws and drive responsibly.

Don’ts

  • Do not smoke inside the vehicle.
  • Do not exceed the mileage limit specified in your contract.
  • Do not allow unauthorized persons to drive the rental.
  • Do not leave personal belongings unattended inside the car.
  • Do not ignore damage notices; report them promptly.
